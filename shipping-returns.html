Shipping & Returns.

Every AMG Society piece is embroidered and printed to order in Paterson, New Jersey — crafted just for you. Here's how it gets to your door, and how we take care of you.

Because each item is made to order, please allow 2–7 business days for us to craft your piece before it ships. Once it's on its way, standard delivery within the U.S. typically takes another 2–5 business days. You'll get a tracking number by email the moment your order ships.

Shipping is free on orders $120 and up. Orders under $120 ship for a flat $6. We currently ship within the United States.

Returns & Exchanges

Every piece is custom-made to order just for you, so we're generally unable to accept returns or exchanges — all sales are final.

That said, we stand fully behind our craftsmanship. If your order arrives with a defect, a production error, or you received the wrong item, reach out within 7 days of delivery and we'll make it right — a repair, a replacement, or a refund. Just send a photo and your order details.

Sizing: because items are made to order, we can't accept returns for fit. Please check our Size Guide before you order — and if you're between sizes or unsure, message us first and we'll help you get it right.

Questions about an order? We've got you. Email amgstudiosnj@gmail.com or DM us on Instagram — include your order number and we'll take care of it.
