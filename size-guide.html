How to Measure

Lay a similar garment flat, or measure yourself

Chest

Measure across the fullest part, from one underarm straight across to the other.

Length

From the highest point of the shoulder straight down to the bottom hem.

Waist

Around the narrowest part of your waist where the waistband sits.

Inseam

From the crotch seam down to the bottom of the leg.
